Coffee First

Coffee drinkers everywhere all share a similar problem: where can I go for good coffee, a good community and a good atmosphere? Coffee First is an app meant to remedy the problem by compiling all of the information a coffee drinker could possibly need, like locations, aesthetics, activities and more. Coffee First is your one stop to see whatâ€™s brewing!

Problem

Coffee Drinkers need a centralized hub of pertinent information for all of their coffee and community needs

Solution

Our team has created an app based solely off this need, meant to help the average coffee drinker find belonging in their vicinity.

The Process

With our app, we started from square one; we didn't have any previous app to work off of. With a lot of User research, we had a great place to start and remedied out lack of a starting point with things like a competitor analysis and a lot of mapping based on our interviews. After all of our research and Ideation, we created a clickable prototype which we ran through another round of testing. After that user testing, we iterated some more and made some better prototypes, integrating some features that our users wanted. After the research and the integration of different features, we ended up with a working, low fidelity prototype.
